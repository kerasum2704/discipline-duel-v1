## 🕹️ How to Play

The game interface shows your stats on the left and your current task queue on the right. Enter your tasks into the input field at the top of the screen. Each task lists a difficulty rating. Higher difficulty ratings grant more experience points upon completion.

### Managing Resilience
Your resilience meter tracks how much stress you can handle. If you ignore tasks, this meter drops. Keep your resilience high by finishing small tasks throughout the day. If your resilience reaches zero, the game triggers a recovery period where you must perform a set of focus-based exercises to resume play.

### Using Counter-strike Mechanics
When a notification interrupts your work, the system simulates an incoming attack. You must acknowledge the interruption and assign a follow-up action to counter it. Successful counters increase your future focus score. This mechanic helps you identify which events cause you to lose momentum.
